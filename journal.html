About the Journal Club

We are an interdisciplinary group of researchers, students, and enthusiasts who meet regularly to discuss foundational and applied work in theoretical biology. Our discussions span topics from mathematical biology to systems biology and beyond.

The journal club is a weekly gathering. One or two members of the club will present a summary of the chosen paper or reading material, that the whole group has read. Then, the discussion will begin. Attendees can ask clarifying questions, inquire about different aspects of the experimental design, critique the methods, and bring a healthy amount of skepticism (or praise) to the results.
